## Rate limits

All API requests are rate-limited. Limits depend on whether you're authenticated:

| Tier | Per hour | Per day | How identity is tracked |
|------|----------|---------|------------------------|
| Anonymous (no API key) | 5 | — | By IP address |
| Registered (free) | 50 | 500 | By user account |

If you exceed the limit, you'll get a `429 Too Many Requests` response. The `X-Rate-Limit-Remaining` header on every response tells your agent how many requests it has left in the current window.

Getting an API key is free — [sign up](https://hermai.ai/register) and create one in the dashboard.

## Credits

Credits are earned through contributions and community activity, and spent on [cloud fetch](/concepts/cloud-fetch) calls.

### Earning

| Action | Credits |
|--------|---------|
| Make your first request | +50 |
| Star [hermai-cli](https://github.com/hermai-ai/hermai-cli) on GitHub | +100 |
| Refer a friend | +100 per invite |
| Contribute a schema | +50 per site |

### Spending

| Call | Credits |
|--------|---------|
| `POST https://api.hermai.ai/v1/fetch` (cloud fetch by URL) | 1 per successful response |
| `POST https://fetch.hermai.ai/v1/fetch` (cloud fetch by name) | 1 per successful response |
| Failed cloud-fetch calls (4xx/5xx after retries) | 0 — reservations are released automatically |

Catalog lookups, registry browsing, and schema pushes are free — they don't consume credits.

## What counts as a request?

Every call to these endpoints counts against your rate limit:

- `GET /v1/catalog/{domain}` (catalog lookup)
- `GET /v1/schemas/{site}/package` (schema download)
- `POST /v1/fetch` on either `api.hermai.ai` or `fetch.hermai.ai`

These do **not** count against rate limits:

- `GET /v1/schemas` (browse/search the registry)
- `GET /v1/schemas/{site}` (public schema card)
- `GET /v1/trending`, `GET /v1/categories` (discovery surfaces)
- `POST /v1/schemas` (pushing a schema)
- `GET /v1/health`

## Usage dashboard

Your [dashboard](https://hermai.ai/dashboard) shows:

- **Requests today** — how many calls you've made
- **Schema hit rate** — how often your lookups found a cached schema
- **Average latency** — response time across your requests
- **Credits earned** — your accumulated credit balance
- **Activity logs** — per-request detail with URL, status, latency, and timestamp

## Free tier today

The free tier covers everything under the rate limits above and includes the credits you earn from contributions and community activity. No credit card required to get started — sign up, contribute a schema, and you can run cloud-fetch calls immediately. Paid plans for higher request volumes and dedicated capacity are on the way; see your [dashboard](https://hermai.ai/dashboard) for current pricing.
